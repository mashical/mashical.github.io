About Our Clinic.

BV PT is based inside **Gym** at Northlakes, Brisbane. BV PT is based on many years of experience with personal training, private practice, sports physiotherapy, aged care, and community physiotherapy to offer the best possible combination of knowledge and skills.

At BV PT we aim to foster a sense of community with our patients, and provide access to quality and affordable physiotherapy services.

BV PT has worked closely with sports teams such as Toa Samoa Rugy League, Auckland based Richmod Rovers and Eden Rugby Union Football Club, providing triage, ongoing assessments, and injury management. These experiences translate into clinical expertise with acute and chronic sporting injuries, strength training and rehabilitation and bio-mechanics.
